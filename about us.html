HISTORY

Krista Bernal, co-owner of San Jose Pharmacy, recognized the absence of a pharmacy in the vicinity of San Jose Rd. in Calamba, Laguna, where numerous eateries existed but no medical establishments were available. This led consumers to travel to distant locations or endure long waits at the nearest Mercury Drug Store.


To address this gap in accessibility, Bernal and the co-owner decided to establish San Jose Pharmacy, naming it after the location. Despite its necessity, the pharmacy faced challenges due to the lack of a dedicated webpage, resulting in limited awareness among potential customers and occasional weak sales.


In response to the sales challenges, the owner had to contribute additional funds to maintain adequate medication stocks. Meanwhile, a colleague of Bernal and the owner, a senior citizen seeking income and occupation, chose to start a business in the Philippines, further contributing to the establishment of San Jose Pharmacy.


San Jose Pharmacy prioritizes serving the local community of Barangay San Jose, offering easier access to medication and providing discounts to residents. This community-focused approach aligns with the pharmacy's mission to address the healthcare needs of its immediate surroundings.
